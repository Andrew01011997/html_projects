Lingua.Live

Helping language lerners grow through conversation via native speakers

PRODUCT

LinguLive fosters student`s practical application of language skills acquired in the classroom by providing a platform for learners to engage in structured conversation with native speakers. We collaborated with secondary language students and profrssional linguists on this project after observing that language learners need a way to practice speaking because the goal of learning a new language is to have real-world conversations.

THE SCOPE

  • Create web responsive platform for students to practice speking the language they are learning with native speakers.

  • Enable students to engage in conversation topics of personal interest.

  • Give students a sense of progress through structured dialogues that leverage knowledge acquired in the classroom.

  • Allow native speakers to provide constructive feedback to foster student`s development

DESIGN PROCESS

Research

This process includes user interviews, competitive analysis, reserch synthesis, persona development, user stories and determination of the information architecture for the product. Below is a quote from one of our user interviews that encompasses a common sentiment of our users.

"The goal of learning a new languages is to be able to have real-world conversations."

Yulia
Rusian Language Student

The problem

After interviwing several language students, we came up with a problem statement that encompasses the key pain point we aimed to solve with design tinking.

Language learners need a way to practice speaking because the goal of learning a new language is to be ablt to have real-word conversations.

Cross platform experience

In order to make the on-demand learning experience more accessible, we deemed web responsivity to be crucial to our users. This allows students to easily have conversations at any time or on the go.
